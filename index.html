About Me

Fidelis Kanyoni

With over 15 years of hands-on experience in the beauty and wellness industry, I’ve had the pleasure of helping countless clients look and feel their best. My work is rooted in a deep passion for self-care and personal transformation, and I specialize in detailed nail care—including manicures, pedicures, and custom nail art—along with expert wig installation and facial beauty treatments. Whether it’s enhancing natural beauty or providing a bold new look, I aim to make every session a confidence boost.

In addition to nail and facial services, I offer professional waxing and relaxing massage therapy, creating a full pampering experience under one roof. My clients appreciate the personal touch, attention to detail, and dedication I bring to every appointment. It's not just about beauty—it's about helping people feel seen, valued, and completely at ease.

You can find me at my workplace at Freedom Heights Mall, Langata Road, Latiface Salon & Spa, designed for comfort and quality service. For clients who prefer the luxury of beauty treatments at home, I’m also mobile and available to travel, within Nairobi, Kiambu, Machako(Syokimau), and Kajiado(Ongata Rongai) counties, offering the same high-level care right at your doorstep. Wherever you are, I’m here to bring the full beauty experience to you.
